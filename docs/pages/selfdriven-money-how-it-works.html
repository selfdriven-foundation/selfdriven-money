Trust By Architecture

A bank that proves, not promises

Traditional banks ask you to trust them. selfdriven.money gives you the tools to verify.

Self-Certifying Identity
Your KERI AID is mathematically bound to your keys. No central authority issues or controls it. If your keys rotate, the cryptographic chain of custody proves continuity. Identity theft becomes architecturally infeasible.
Verifiable Credentials
Every claim — your KYC status, account ownership, transaction authority — is an ACDC verifiable credential. Regulators can verify data provenance cryptographically. Your statements are attested documents, not just PDFs.
Phishing-Proof Authentication
FIDO2 passkeys eliminate passwords and SMS codes entirely. Your biometric is verified locally on your device. The authentication assertion is bound to the specific origin — relay and phishing attacks fail by design.
Cryptographic Audit Trail
Every transaction you sign creates a KERI interaction event with a SHA-256 digest. Your key event log is witnessed by a distributed network. The audit trail is append-only and tamper-evident. We can\u2019t alter your records — and neither can anyone else.
